Congratulations, Team Swayat from VNIT Nagpur on qualifying for Hurocup, FIRA 2016!!

﻿​​﻿IvLabs  is proud to announce the qualification of Team Swayat in the HuroCup event of  FIRA 2016. The mega event will be held from 12th to 18th December in Beijing, China. All the walking and computer vision algorithms were tested on a  research platform, NAO from Aldebaran Robotics. Swayat is the only  team from India which will be competing  against teams from more than ten nations around the world.​
